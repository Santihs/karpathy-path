# Linear Algebra Basics

> Seed note — fill in after completing 3Blue1Brown Essence of Linear Algebra.

## Core concepts to capture

- Vectors as arrows in space vs. as lists of numbers — why both views matter
- Matrix multiplication as composed transformations
- Dot product as a measure of similarity / projection
- Embeddings: why words/tokens can be represented as vectors in high-dimensional space

## Why it matters for AI

Matrix multiplications are the core operation of every neural network layer (`y = Wx + b`). Embeddings are the language of LLMs. Understanding these geometrically is what separates "I run the code" from "I understand what the code does."
